Student Careers at NCSU

Campus Enterprises at NC State University oversees key departments that enhance campus life. The Campus Enterprises Administration strategically coordinates various operations. NC State Dining focuses on a sustainable, nutritious, and innovative dining experience. NC State Student Centers manages spaces for community engagement and social activities. NC State Wolf Xpress ensures smooth campus transportation and logistics, overseeing the Wolfline bus system and package delivery services. Wolfpack Outfitters is the premier retail destination, offering a variety of merchandise showcasing NC State pride. The WolfPack Outfitters/NC State Bookstore is a hub for textbooks, academic resources, and campus identity. Together, these departments create a vibrant, inclusive environment, shaping the collegiate experience at NC State University.
